Café

  • What will make me the most buzzed? (When the barista asks you what to order and you already know what you want.)

Dinner Restaurant

  • Hi, _____. Thanks, _____. (When the waiter tells you his/her name.)
  • Which wine goes well with food? (When the waiter asks about beverages.)
  • Awful. Send it back. (When sampling the wine.)
  • It's awful. (When asked how the food is.)

Bar

  • Can I see IDs? … Just wanted to see if you're as cute in your picture. (When talking to girls near entrance.)
  • I'm trying to avoid someone. Quick, let's have a fake conversation. Go. (When accidentally bumping into someone.)

Club

  • ID, please. (When waiting outside a club with people.)
  • Oh, the wife is going to have a riot when I tell her I was ID'd. (When getting ID'd outside a club.)

Work

  • Went on a cruise to Hawaii. And by a cruise to Hawaii I mean suntaned on my balcony all weekend and drank mojitos. (When asked what you did the past weekend.)

Dinner Party

  • I'm _____. I'm sure you've heard all about me.
